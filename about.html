About:

Tears of Themis is a free-to-play adventure game developed by miHoYo, published by miHoYo in mainland China and worldwide by Cognosphere, HoYoverse. The game was first released in mainland China with Simplified Chinese language in July 2020 for Android and iOS.

In Tears of Themis, a detective adventure of love and wits, you are a new attorney at law on a journey to solve unusual cases together with your destined partner.

About Luke:

Luke Pearce (Raven) is a character who appears in the game Tears of Themis and its derivative works produced by HoYoVerse. He is a childhood friend of the Main Character, the founder of Pearce Private Investigations. and owner of Time's Antiquities, an antique store that serves as a front to his private investigation business.

Luke is a blonde, hazel-eyed young man of fair complexion. He wears a yellow t-shirt, a brown denim button-down, an olive jacket, the latter two being unbuttoned, and a pair of denim jeans. Around his neck, he wears a key in the manner of a necklace, and a camera fastened by a strap.

About Artem:

Artem Wing (Libra) is a character who appears in the game Tears of Themis and its derivative works produced by HoYoVerse. Graduated from Stellis University, he obtained his doctorate at the age of 25, and was qualified as a senior attorney at the age of 28 with a win rate of 99%. He is a chief and senior attorney in Themis Law Firm as well as Main Character's co-worker.

Artem has brown hair and blue eyes. During work, he wears a 3-piece suit with matching sapphire cufflinks and a tie clip.

About Vyn:

Vyn Richter (Adjucator, born Vilhelm Richard Albert de Haspran) is a character who appears in the game Tears of Themis and its derivative works produced by HoYoVerse. He is a well-known psychologist, hypnotist, the co-owner and chief researcher of Giannovyn Mental Health Research Center, and a visiting professor at the School of Psychology of Stellis University.

Vyn has white hair and golden eyes, due to being of mixed blood. He dresses primarily in vintage European-style clothing consisting of a beige vest over a white shirt with sleeve garters.

About Marius:

Marius von Hagen (King) is a character who appears in the game Tears of Themis and its derivative works produced by HoYoVerse. He is the second son of the current head of the von Hagen family, Austin von Hagen, and the executive president of the Pax Group.

Marius is a tall young man with purple-toned hair, purple eyes, and fair skin. He is typically seen wearing several piercings, a black logo t-shirt, a white button-down overshirt and denim pants. He wears two necklaces, the foremost visible one being a dog tag inscribed with the letter "Z" and a pendant necklace hidden under his shirt.
